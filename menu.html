Meet Our Chef

Chef Antoine Dubois

With over 20 years of experience in Michelin-starred kitchens across Paris and New York, Chef Dubois brings a passion for classic French technique and modern innovation to The Gilded Spoon.

His philosophy is simple: let the quality of the ingredients speak for itself. He personally curates our seasonal menu, ensuring every dish is a memorable experience of flavor, texture, and art.

"Food is not just sustenance. It is an experience, a memory, a form of art. My goal is to create that art for every guest."

The Gilded Spoon

Delivering an unmatched fine dining experience since 2010. We are dedicated to artful cuisine and exceptional service.
